2023 Undergraduate Student Service Award
Awarded by the
University of California, San Diego
The 2023 Undergraduate Student Service Award is a prestigious honor presented to a select group of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) students who have gone above and beyond in their roles as student leaders. These individuals have made significant contributions to creating a better environment for their peers through exceptional dedication, innovative ideas, and inspiring leadership.
