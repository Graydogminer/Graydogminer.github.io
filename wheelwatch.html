WheelWatch - Smart Bike Lock
Developed at the
University of California, San Diego
WheelWatch is an innovative smart bike U-lock that enhances security by detecting tampering using an accelerometer for movement detection and a wire embedded in the "U" shackle. When tampering is detected, it triggers an alarm and sends real-time notifications to users via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). At its core, the ESP32 microcontroller integrates dual-core processing with built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth to enable seamless communication between the lock's sensors, buzzer, and the user's smartphone. The IoT integration allows users to monitor and control their bike lock through a user-friendly interface, enabling features such as remote arming, disarming, and receiving safety alerts. This comprehensive system combines hardware and software to ensure robust bike protection. Learn more about WheelWatch here.
